T-Shirt Design Project

Design a plasma character for a Minecraft T-Shirt with adjacent "PlasmCraft.org" text.

Design should extend behind periodic table of elements. Design will reside on a dark blue shirt (almost black).

Utilize only 6 colors, including a white base, for t-shirt screen printing.

Create 10 block with patterns using 6 colors. (We will place blocks in chart using our javascript layout to save you time.)

3D object file preferred for character. Work may be assembled as a high resolution (300 dpi) layered Photoshop file at least 10 inches wide.

Also provide any vector files used (Illustrator, SVG or EPS).


About the character

Plasmorpheustm is calm and cool, despite his/her hot plasma form. Radiating with electricity, her colors include the blinding white of lightning, and the orange and red of fire. Dark spaces, purples and blues are also visible on his edges and in the moving electricity that cooly sparks and slowly arcs around him.

A contemporary form of the Roman God Morpheus, Plasmorpheus is a god of dreams with the ability to emulate multiple forms. At his most advanced state, Plasmorpheus may look like a winged angel composed of pure plasma.

Subtle moving energy surrounds Plasmorpheus, like the arcing electricity of a Tesla coil. These webs and feeders of energy should extend behind the periodic table of elements. A torus shape creates the impression of wings.

Plasmorpheus exudes a Christlike/Buddhalike calmness, despite his potential to unleash unfathomable power. The position on his hands or the shape of her body should convey this level of tranquility in the presence of great power.
