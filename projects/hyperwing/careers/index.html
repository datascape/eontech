Careers

Aerospace Engineer

Aerospace engineers are constantly tweaking the design of HyperWing to make it fast and fuel efficient, both in the tube and in the air.


Georgia Schools offering Degrees in Arospace Engineering

  • • Georgia Tech | School of Aerospace Engineering
  • • Middle Georgia Collage | Aircraft Structural Technology (A.A.S.)


Mechanical Engineer

Mechanical engineers construct, supervise, operate, and maintain the wing and hyperloop. They also assemble parts to repair the wing with our robot team.

  • • Georgia Tech | George W. Woodruff School of Mechanical Engineering
  • • Southern Polytechnic | Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering
  • • University of Georgia | Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering
  • • Georgia Southern | Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering
  • • Mercer University | Master's Program in Mechanical Engineering


Civil Engineer

Civil engineers work with city, state and local goverment to add launch areas and safe flightways. They also design the layout of Technology City neighborhoods to allow for the fastest, safe delivery routes seperate from slow vehicle traffic.


  • • Columnbia | Masters of Civil Engineering and Engineering Mechanics
  • • Southern Polytechnic | Civil and Construction Engineering
  • • University of Georgia | Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering
  • • Georgia Tech | School of Civil and Environmental Engineering
